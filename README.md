## CampusQueue_System
## Project Overview: 
To develop a digital queue management system that allows students to join, monitor and manage queues remotely, improving efficiency and reducing waiting times on campus.

***Problem Statement:***
Traditional physical queuing on campus often leads to long wait times, overcrowded hallways, and decreased student productivity. Students frequently spend hours standing in line for services like registration, financial aid, or academic advising without knowing when they will be served.

***The Solution:***
CampusQueue_System is a web/mobile-based platform designed to digitize the queuing experience. By providing real-time data and remote access, upon joining a queue, the system generates a unique Ticket Number(e.g., A-102) linked to the student’s profile, sends a notification when the student is about to be served. The system empowers students to manage their time more effectively while allowing campus administrators to optimize staff allocation based on live traffic data.
